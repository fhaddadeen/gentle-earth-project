Project Portal

Transparency at Every Stage

The GEP Project Portal gives registered stakeholders secure access to project-level documentation, monitoring data, verification reports, and progress updates across our active and upcoming projects.

Access is role-based — what you see depends on your relationship to the project. Investors, carbon credit buyers, local communities, governments, and certification entities each have tailored dashboards.

  • Real-time ecosystem monitoring data
  • Carbon and biodiversity credit verification reports
  • Project progress updates and phase milestones
  • Financial documentation for registered investors
  • Community engagement records and consultation logs
  • Satellite and aerial observation data

For access enquiries, contact contact@gentleearthproject.com
