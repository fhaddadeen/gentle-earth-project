Sahara Sunday Spain

Founder & CEO

A legal specialist and entrepreneur with over a decade of experience in contract law and complex project structuring, Sahara Sunday Spain has spent her life navigating the intersection of development, ecology, and long-term accountability.

Published her first book at nine and founded a school for girls in Mali that operated for over thirteen years. She has lived in more than twenty countries and holds two degrees from Colgate University.

Sahara founded Gentle Earth Project on the conviction that responsible development requires not just good intentions, but durable structures — systems designed to stay accountable to land, communities, and outcomes long after the work becomes less visible.
